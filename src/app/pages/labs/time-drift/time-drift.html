Time Drift

← Back to Labs

Time passes whether your app is open or not. This lab shows how to measure and use that gap.

What it does

We store a checkpoint timestamp in localStorage, then compare it to the current time. The difference is the drift: the window you can use for offline progression, staleness checks, and delayed work.

What it’s meant to teach

  • Derived state: drift is computed from two timestamps, not stored.
  • Persistence: the checkpoint survives reloads because it’s saved.
  • Catch-up logic: many apps convert drift into work completed “while away”.
